01 : About me

A little about myself...

Hello, my name is Keya Patel, I am a Grade 8 student, studying at Tredway Woodsworth Public School in Scarborough, ON. I have a profound passion for both writing and painting. My vibrant imagination and creative spirit shine through her work, whether I am crafting compelling stories or bringing canvas to life with my artistic talents. Beyond my academic pursuits, I am eager to make a positive impact on my community, actively seeking opportunities to get involved in community initiatives and lend a helping hand wherever I can. My dedication to both my studies and community involvement reflects my commitment to personal growth and making a difference in the world around me.

What its like working as a PAGE:

As a proud participant in the Ontario Page Program, I am thrilled to be part of a unique opportunity that allows students like me to engage directly with the democratic process. In this role, my responsibilities extend to supporting the daily operations of the Legislative Assembly, where I have the privilege of assisting Members of Provincial Parliament (MPPs) and witnessing firsthand the legislative proceedings. From distributing documents to providing assistance during debates, my tasks are diverse and essential to the smooth functioning of the parliamentary environment. This program not only offers valuable insights into the inner workings of government but also fosters a sense of civic responsibility and an appreciation for the democratic principles that shape our province. It's an incredible journey of learning, growth, and civic engagement that I am proud to be a part of.
