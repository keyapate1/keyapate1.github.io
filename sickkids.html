Painting for SickKids

For this initiative, my brother and I created paintings and sold them to potential buyers to raise money for SickKids Foundation. I always had a vision for helping people in need and felt that SickKids foundation was perfect for this project. I created several paintings with acrylic paint, learning different techniques from my older brother and from YouTube through research. In the 2 months in the summer of 2023 that we ran this initiative, the project was able to raise over $500 dollars with paintings selling for $10, $20, $25, and $50. In order to gain attraction for the project, I created an Instagram page @an_artistic_m1ndset where I would post photos of the art that I created and share it with the small following of supporters.
